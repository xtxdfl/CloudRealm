# CloudRealm
It is a new-generation operation and management platform that deeply integrates big data operation and maintenance (Big DataOps), artificial intelligence (AI), and the full-process management philosophy of DevOps.Based on big data technology as its foundation, the platform aggregates full-dimensional operation and maintenance data, including logs, monitoring, tracing, configurations, release records, and more.Powered by AI algorithms as its core engine, it enables intelligent analysis, root cause localization, anomaly prediction, and automated decision-making in operation and maintenance scenarios.With DevOps principles as its process framework, it connects the entire lifecycle of development, testing, and operation and maintenance.Ultimately, it upgrades traditional "passive responsive operation and maintenance" to "proactive predictive, automated, and intelligent operation and maintenance", greatly improving operation efficiency, reducing failure risks, and accelerating business delivery.
